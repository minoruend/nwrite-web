Designed for writers who value organization

Nwrite integrates structure and creative freedom, keeping your plot details alongside your text editor.

Structured Text Editor

Write your chapters and scenes in a clean, aesthetic environment. Includes automatic saving every 5 seconds to ensure you never lose a word.

Character Profiles

Track character traits, background stories, descriptions, and assign custom icons to form detailed digital character sheets.

World & Lore Manager

Build and categorize lore about your fictional universe. Map out important locations, history, magical systems, and plot points.

Relationship Graphs

Easily map and visualize the complicated connections, family trees, alliances, and rivalries between your characters.

Everything you need, organized in tabs

Nwrite keeps a minimal, clutter-free tabbed layout so you can jump from writing to planning instantly.

Distraction-free Writing Mode
